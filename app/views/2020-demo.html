What we can achieve between April 2019 and April 2020

This is the vision we took to GDS alpha assessment & received programme approval for - with the last 6 months of learning and insights folded in.

Since September we had two objectives, what did we prove/learn?

We have

  • demonstrated the benefits of a single, up-to-date, estate plan - by operating a private beta service. We recommend: scale to all plans.
  • established digital housing development data (CAD) can be used to create title plans. We recommend: proceed to use in a trial.

What does this proposition demonstrate?

The public platform for estate plans we have built, validated, and operate.

Prototype parts to complete a vision to maximise value - getting CAD; operating a live service for all submitted and approved estate plans

Who is this for and what are the benefits?

Housing Developers, Conveyancers - using the service, to be right at completion (submitting OS2's and applications)

Staff: approving plans - no additional overhead; OS2 applications - no checking and correcting. Title plans created using CAD

Information available for Lenders, Ordnance Survey, buyers, and other interested parties.

  • everything about the plan is right before completion
  • everyone has access to the authoritative, up-to-date, plan, when it's needed
  • all current estate plans online - if they are active
  • all new and future estate plans online - via new submission process
  • no additional work for staff in approving and publishing
  • digital development data (CAD) - for many estates - private beta
  • OS2 forms are correct when submitted - no manual checking and correcting is required
  • Deed plans do not need rectification post completion
