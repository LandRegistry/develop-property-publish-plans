Beta Proposition

Narratives:

I'm a developer and I want to check that all properties on my development plan can be registered

Features:

  • Check my development plan - use CAD from the developer to complete extent checks, and automate plot extent checks and creation.
  • Get notifications - send email notifications to subscribers when the estate plan changes.
  • View estate information - provide information to everyone involved in buying and selling new build properties.

What is our MVP? (minimum viable product)

  • first release: housing development information and estate plan with a permanent, shareable URL.
    • housing development (GOV.UK)
    • housing development (no brand)
  • first iteration: list of housing developments with development information and estate plan - each development has a unique, permanent and shareable URL.
    • housing developments (GOV.UK)
    • housing developments (no brand)

A future vision of housing development information

Information about new housing developments will be publically available from the planning stage until their sale. This could include up to 10000 housing developments and over 175,000 properties.

What happens after sale?


We have a Find Property information service - which allows anyone to see a property record.

Property records were investigated in GDS/Land Registry - Exemplar #5 Post Exemplar we thought about creating a citizen readable register - a permanent, public, page for each property in England and Wales (26 Million).

Property pages could be used as:

  • stand alone property records - a full record of each property including type, location and restrictions of use. This could be like a property 'logbook' or history
  • property information pages that could be used as part of a transactional journey. For example, to buy or sell; re-mortgage; transfer ownership after death
  • a reduced property page like page per property on gov.uk (5 Granary) that collates everything useful from across GOV and local government such as bin collection and council tax bands

These would be built as registers or APIs so they could be used inside GOV and by industry in the UK.
