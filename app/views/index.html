I want to develop land or property

Ready to sell: beta proposition

This prototype shows the features we intend to investigate and create during the first 6 months of private beta.

The prototype will change as we learn more from users.

Beta Proposition

Narratives:

I'm a developer and I want to check that all properties on my development plan can be registered

Features:

  • Check my development plan - use CAD from the developer to complete extent checks, and automate plot extent checks and creation.
  • Get notifications - send email notifications to subscribers when the estate plan changes.
  • View estate information - provide information to everyone involved in buying and selling new build properties.

What is our MVP? (minimum viable product)

  • first release: housing development information and estate plan with a permanent, shareable URL.
    • housing development (GOV.UK)
    • housing development (no brand)
  • first iteration: list of housing developments with development information and estate plan - each development has a unique, permanent and shareable URL.
    • housing developments (GOV.UK)
    • housing developments (no brand)

Where do Housing Developments / Homes belong? GOV.UK?

What would we like to know by the end of private beta?

175,000 New build homes per year (underestimate). 4000 Housing developments (estimation).

Whilst being planned, built and up to the point of first sale, 'Develop' content will be available for the Seller, Buyer, their agents (conveyancers), Mortgage Lenders, etc.

Where do these 175,000 pieces of content go - now, and after sale? We have a Find Property information service - which allows anyone to see a property record

And... The GDS/Land Registry - Exemplar #5 - investigated this (amongst other things), and post Exemplar we thought about a citizen readable register. Permanent, public, page(s) for each property in England and Wales (26 Million).

Property pages could have use as:

  • stand alone property records / information about the property/home - a full record of What it is, Where it is; What you can and can't do with it; What others can do with it - like a 'property manual/logbook/history'
  • property information pages/content that could be used as part of a transactional or step-by-step journey. eg buy/sell; mortgage; borrow against; transfer ownership after death.
  • a cut down property page that collates everything else useful about the place - from across GOV and local GOV - 'page per property on gov.uk'(5 Granary) - local and gov services like bins and council tax bands
  • and, obvs, all as Registers, API's, etc - so can be used inside GOV and by industry in the UK
