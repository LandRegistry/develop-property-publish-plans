I want to develop land or property - Alpha

Prototypes of, and thinking about: Services and User Journeys for developing, selling, and buying 'new build' homes in England and Wales.

Exploring concepts of what could be. Making examples to provoke conversations about high level ideas of what could be done, and what can't/shouldn't be.

This is a wide look, across many things, not a deep dive into any particular service/solution.
