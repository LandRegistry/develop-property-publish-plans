User research themes

A summary of user research on the features we are building.

Notifications

User research from buyers’ conveyancers has shown:

  • they want notifications via email and are happy to provide an email address
  • they don’t want to log in to something but would sign up ‘straight away’ to an email notification service

Buyers' conveyancers need notifications to:

  • be informed of plan withdrawal so they have time to act on it
  • be informed of any plan change ahead of lodging an OS2
  • have confirmation that it is the most recent plan

On average, buyers' conveyancers are told of 50% of changes by the building developer. However:

  • some building developers send out more notifications
  • notifications of changes to the plan can be very late
  • although most changes are small, critical changes can cause major problems for the buyers' conveyancer

Feedback about example notifications from buyers' conveyancers showed that they:

  • want to know the name of the housing development in the subject line
  • liked plot level details in the email body
  • didn’t understand the term ‘personal information charter’
