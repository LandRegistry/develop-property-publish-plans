Check my development plan

I'm a developer and I want to check my plans: to develop land into homes to sell.

These prototypes show how we could take the plans a developer has, the estate layout drawings, and precedent deeds, other supporting documents and...

Create provisional new titles - create the plot layout, draw up the register entries; determine if this title would be registrable; publish all this information, and inform the developer of any problems we foresee with registration

Example situation, told via prototypesVersion 1, Wed, 9th May

  1. I'm a developer and a want to check my estate plans wont result in unregistrable (unsaleable) plots/properties
  2. two days later, HM Land Registry have finished checking registrability of the plots/properties, and
    • published pages for the estate and the plots
    • notified the developer that a some plots are currently unregistrable
  3. some time passes, and the developer submits a revised plan for the estate
  4. two days later, HM Land Registry have finished checking registrability of the plots, and
    • updated pages for the estate and the plots
    • notified the developer*

* notifications: probably also notify Ordnance Survey, and anyone else registered for notifications.
