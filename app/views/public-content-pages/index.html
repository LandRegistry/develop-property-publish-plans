About the development

Public content about the development (estate).

These pages show how we could publish all the information we know about an estate. Where it is, who is developing it, what it consists of - plots/properties. What 'state' those plots/properties are in... Be as transparent as possible.

Although not yet shown, we should think of these pages as being capable of showing everything government knows about the estate/plots/properties. For example there must be a public document about planning permission.

What public content looks like
Being transparent means everyone can see what the current state of the plot or property is; they can choose to 'watch it' - to be notified of any change, and to be notified if there is a problem. This one, current, view of things, replaces the multiple copies of plans and other documents that exist and the processes between parties to keep checking the state of the development before taking positive actions

  • I'm a developer and i've just had the first version of my plans checked - what does a registrable estate look like after first check?
  • I'm a developer and i've just had the first version of my plans checked - and there is a problem with a plot - what does a registrable estate look like after first check?
