Check housing development plan

This service is for housing developers, building 10 or more properties.

Use this service to:

  • check if your planned development is on land you own
  • check all plots extents to see if any would be unregistrable
  • be notified of any potential problems with registration of plot extents
  • provide buyers conveyancers with all property plan information they need to register the property

If you have a digital plan this service can provide:

  • faster plan checks
  • fewer surveying issues during development
  • faster registration and fewer problems post completion

Before you start

You will need:

  • to be developing ten or more properties - these can be new or a conversion of an existing property
  • a PDF plan or digital estate layout plan
